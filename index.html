About Me

With over 25 years of teaching experience and 15 years in research, I am passionate about advancing the frontiers of embedded systems, FPGA-based solutions, and intelligent automation. My Ph.D. research focused on "Low-Power, High-Performance Algorithms for Sleep Apnea Detection," combining healthcare technology with cutting-edge system design.

Currently serving as Associate Professor and Program Head at MIT Vishwaprayag University, I lead initiatives in Edge AI solutions and state-of-the-art laboratory development. My work spans across multiple domains including Electronics, Mechatronics, Automation, and Computer Science.
